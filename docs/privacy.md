# Privacy

NUUL processes files on your device only. No uploads, no accounts, no third-party analytics by default. Receipt Vault is optional and stores receipts locally in your browser.

## What NUUL does

- Reads metadata locally.
- Runs OCR locally to detect common leaks.
- Re-encodes output to remove metadata.

## What NUUL does not do

- It does not upload images to any server.
- It does not store images unless you explicitly save exports.
- It does not guarantee anonymity; it reduces common leaks.
